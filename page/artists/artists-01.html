Gyeong Yoon An

안경윤

Hee Welling is a Danish furniture designer who founded his own studio in Copenhagen in 2003. Welling has received a number of design awards, including the Bo Bedre Award (Denmark), Good Design Award (USA) and has been nominated as the Danish designer of the year in both 2013 and 2014. Welling is the son of a cabinetmaker, and his fascination with production techniques, materials and machinery was fired as a child. Education

2009 MFA in Sculpture, Slade School of Fine Art, UCL, London
2008 MFA in Fine Art, Seoul National University, Seoul
2005 BFA in Fine Art, Seoul National University, Seoul

Selected Exhibition

2009 Interim Show, Woburn Research center, London
2008 Degree Show, Seoul National University, Seoul
2007 Wonderyou, Woosuk Hall, Seoul (group show)
2006 Fever, Gallery Wooduk, Seoul (group show)
